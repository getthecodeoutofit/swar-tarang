About swar Tarang

The heartbeat of musical excellence at GEHU Bhimtal

swar Tarang, established in 2024, is the premier music club of Graphic Era Hill University, Bhimtal. We are a vibrant community of over 50 passionate musicians who believe in the power of music to unite, inspire, and transform.

As the best band in the university, we have earned over 20 trophies and continue to set new standards in musical excellence. Our club provides a platform for students to explore their musical talents, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and create unforgettable memories.
